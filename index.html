Audio samples

This page presents audio samples for our submission Spatially Selective Speaker Separation Using a DNN With a Location Dependent Feature Extraction.

They demonstrate the difference between using a real-valued masking to enhance only the magnitude vs. additionally performing a phase enhancement.
All results are obtained from the same trained model (high-complexity setup of CRUSE with location dependent feature extraction)

Major experiment parameters:
  • Uniform rectangular array with 9 microphones (4.2cm distance between neighboring microphones)
  • Background noise was added at SNR=0dB
  • Time-invariant source and target DOAs
Otherwise as described in our submission.
